Our Story

From Startup to Global Technology Leader

Founded in 2010 in Silicon Valley, NexaCorp began with a simple mission: to make enterprise technology accessible, scalable, and transformative. What started as a five-person consulting firm has grown into a global powerhouse with offices in 12 countries and a team of 1,200+ professionals.

Our journey has been defined by an unwavering commitment to innovation and a deep understanding of our clients' needs. We have successfully delivered over 2,000 projects, helping businesses across industries navigate the complexities of digital transformation.

Today, NexaCorp stands as a trusted partner for Fortune 500 companies, government agencies, and ambitious startups alike, delivering solutions that drive measurable business outcomes.
